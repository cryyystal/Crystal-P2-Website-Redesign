Obtain an E-Card

The Windsor Public Library is pleased to offer electronic cards (eCards). An eCard is an option for those who need a new card and canâ€™t visit a bricks and mortar branch.
They will provide access to online resources, including streaming media, eBooks, audio-eBooks, newspapers, magazines, and others.
They allow holds to be placed in BiblioCommons. The eCard can be converted to a regular card in-branch when items are checked out.

Exceptions:

    Card cannot be used to check out physical items.
    Card cannot be used to log onto Library computers.

Rules to getting an E-Card:

    Be a resident of Windsor/Essex County.
    Be at least 13 years of age.




To obtain an eCard, an applicant must complete and submit the online registration form below.
When an application is approved, library staff will email the library card number and PIN to the applicant.
All applications will be responded to within 48 business hours.
Submitting the application below indicates acceptance of these conditions.
